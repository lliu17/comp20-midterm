Great Lakes Gallery is dedicated to the preservation and appreciation of the nature of the Great Lakes region. Home to more than 30 million people, 2500 species of animals, and 18% of the world’s freshwater supply, the Great Lakes region is undoubtedly a global treasure. For years, artists have been drawn to the Great Lakes, inspired by the lush flora and fauna to create an impressive portfolio of works that capture organic splendor of the environment and its inhabitants.

As part of our mission, we are committed to working to preserve and protect the Great Lakes. Therefore, ___% of all profits go to The Alliance for the Great Lakes, a nonpartisan nonprofit working across the region to protect our most precious resource through advocacy, action, and analysis.

To learn more about The Alliance for the Great Lakes, go to greatlakes.org.
